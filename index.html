WHAT IS AUCTUS PROJECT?

The Auctus Project was created with the mission of Improving the pension market by increasing transparency and eliminating common problems such as corporate governance issues, corruption, fraud, bribery, bureaucracy as well as lowering operational costs.

Through smart contract auditable code, the platform will ensure full transparency and compliance to the fund rules and also allow calculations for contributions, employer match and benefits as well as other features, such as investment profiles, beneficiaries designation (in case of death), administration fees calculations and portability between funds.
