Smart Contracts powered Pension Funds

The Auctus Platform will be a global smart contracts based platform for pension funds.

Through smart contract auditable code the platform will ensure full transparency and compliance.


Download the whitepaper

Advantages

Smart contracts with pension funds terms and conditions that assure trust in the enforceability of the contract

Fully Transparent

Easily auditable and compliant

Tansaction activity record that cannot be altered and is easily auditable.

No hidden fees

Funds can’t charge hidden fees. Know exactly what you are paying in fees.

More flexible and scalable infrastructure

Resulting in economies of scale with reduced administrative costs.

Transfer your pension

Easy and fast portability to another pension fund.

Faster processing for retirement, death benefits

Automatically triggers payments at retirement or release funds to the chosen beneficiary on death.

Roadmap

  • Q3 2017

    • Initial Token sale
    • Partnerships with exchanges

  • Q3 2017

    Development of first Smart contract-Powered Pension Fund (Auctus Retirement Fund)

  • Q1 2018

    • Onboarding of traditional assets
    • Fiat payment gateway integration
    • Hiring specialized law firms across the major markets

  • Q2 2018

    • Development of Auctus Platform
    • Partnerships with traditional pension funds for smart-contract customization

  • Q3 2018

    • Launch of Auctus Platform com utilização por fundos tradicionais
    • Expand Auctus Platform adoption in main markets

  • Q4 2018

    • Launch of Annuities Marketplace
    • Pension-backed loans scheme development

  • Further Developments

    • Integrated risk management tool for pension funds
    • Solutions for financial advisers
    • Self Invested Personal Pension scheme
