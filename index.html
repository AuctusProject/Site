MARKETPLACE

FINANCIAL EXPERTS

Financial advisors can earn AUC by providing specialized newsletters, portfolio recommendations, live chats or over the phone support.

Customers can access on-demand human advice via in-app messaging or over the phone using AUC tokens.

Where the nature of the service involves portfolio suggestions and predictions, users will pay only when predictions are correct.

THIRD-PARTY ROBO-ADVISORS

Third-Party Robo-Advisor providers will be able to earn AUC tokens by providing bespoke investment strategies.

Customers can access different investment strategies with blockchain-based proven track records.

The transfer of AUC tokens between the user and the provider can be bound to the results by using smart contracts and recording the predictions on the Ethereum blockchain.
